Intro

Hello, I am Alexandre Carbonell, a professional mathematician with a Master's degree in Applied Mathematics from ETH Zürich and a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from EPFL. I am based in Zürich, Switzerland, originally from Lyon, France. Currently, I work as an Associate - Pricing Analyst at Ageas Re, focusing on statistical modeling, quantitative analysis, and financial engineering within the insurance and reinsurance sectors.

Work

At Ageas Re, I specialize in developing precise pricing models and risk management strategies. My work involves extensive statistical modeling, quantitative analysis, and financial engineering to assess and manage risks in the insurance and reinsurance sectors. My expertise allows me to contribute effectively to the company's analytical and strategic needs.
